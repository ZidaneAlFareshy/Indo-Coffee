The pearl lobster, also known as Panulirus ornatus, is a premium tropical spiny lobster species renowned for its vibrant, striking appearance and exceptional culinary quality. Sourced from pristine waters, this lobster features a beautifully colored exoskeleton with shades of green, blue, and yellow, and is prized for its tender, flavorful meat.

Panulirus ornatus is sought after in high-end seafood markets and gourmet restaurants for its rich, succulent texture and versatility in various dishes, from grilled lobster tails to luxurious seafood platters. Its impressive size and distinctive look make it a showpiece in any meal. We ensure the highest standards of sustainability and quality, delivering frozen or live pearl lobsters to meet the needs of international buyers.

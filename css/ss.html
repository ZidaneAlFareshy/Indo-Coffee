About Us

Indo Aquatic Trade has been a leading seafood manufacturing company in Indonesia since 2021. We are a renowned enterprise devoted to the production and exportation of superior-grade marine products. We exclusively collaborate with local Indonesian fishermen to provide top-notch seafood for worldwide distribution.
